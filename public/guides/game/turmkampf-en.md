# Tower Battle

Tower battles will occur in GW to clear paths or secure territory. Either enemy attacks on our towers, or we need to fight our way to a building.

## Time Game

Tower battles are primarily a time game. Raid leaders are not always online, or raids cannot always be filled. In those phases, you need to play for time.

Important: Destroying towers takes longer than building or repairing them. Once a tower is occupied by an enemy, a percentage starts running showing the fire status.

If a tower burns to 100%, repairs are severely slowed for 12 hours — this must be avoided at all costs.

## Bubble Talent

Either defeat the enemy, or protect the tower using a GW talent Bubble. Talent: Ceasefire in the Development tree — requires 115 talent points, 72 hour cooldown.

The bubble lasts 60 minutes, then the tower cannot be protected for 8 hours. Use this talent with planning, and only on orders from R4/R5.

## Defending Our Towers

Clicking on the tower shows you the enemy units — scouting is therefore not necessary. Towers also provide a few percent defensive bonus.

## Destroying Enemy Towers — Cat and Mouse

Destroying towers also requires a time game. Raid leaders try to get the relevant towers into a burning state — through raids with players who have the demolition buff from GW talents activated.

When multiple players are present, several towers can be attacked as a distraction. Just before a defending raid arrives, the demolition is cancelled. This ties up enemy troops and players. We call it "Cat and Mouse".

The success of tower battles often depends not on the strength of raid leaders, but on the number of players and their patience. A tower battle can easily last several hours.

## Important Rules

- During the tower battle, clan chat is ONLY for battle communication. No gifts, no conversations, no raids on street forces or police.
- ALWAYS send one T4 Bruiser with every march — otherwise the troop speed reveals what is in the raid.
- Callouts like 4/3/3 always mean the ratio of Bruiser/Hitmen/Bikes.

## Troop Callouts in Raids

When a defending raid is started, the leader decides whether to take it. Important: do not all leave the tower at once — a quick solo attack becomes possible. You have 5 minutes after the raid starts. Stay calm.

We mainly use English terms for faster communication:

- Bruiser or Knifes = Bruisers
- Hitmen = Gunners
- Even out = equal distribution 33/33/33
