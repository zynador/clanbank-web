# Formations

Formations only work when all three troop types are in your lineup. You will see 4 small groups of each troop type in the formations.

You can see this clearly by going to the Weapons Trade building, clicking the "i" in the top right, then selecting Strategy.

For reference: "Formation" = Square, "Flanke" = Wedge

Each small group is led by a matching hero. For raids with one troop type, it makes sense to use four troop heroes and one hero with general stats.

## Battle Lines

Think of formations as battle lines fighting against enemy lines. In the first and third rows, the small groups stand side by side — they fight simultaneously and increase total damage.

In the second row, two small groups stand one behind the other. The rear group only fights when the front group is defeated.

With Wedge formations, only the third row is in a line. This is why fewer troops die in wedge formations — two rows still provide reinforcements. Wedges are better for safe victories with fewer losses.

## The Three Waves

Battles run in three waves — like a dice game. In wave one, the defender attacks the first row, then his first row gets attacked. In wave two, both second rows fight. In wave three, everyone fights. If a row is completely defeated, the third row always steps in as a replacement.

## Pure Troop Attack — 5 T3 Disruption Troops

That is why we send 5 T3 units of another troop type into a pure troop attack.

Example — pure biker attack using Gunner formation:

- Wave 1: 5 dead T3 Gunners, counter-attack from Bikes (row 1 gone)
- Wave 2: 5 dead T3 Bruisers, counter-attack from Bikes (row 2 gone)
- Wave 3: full Biker attack — bikes stand at the back and fight in all three waves

## Choosing Formation — Based on Your Troops

- Bruiser attack: Biker Formation or Gunner Wedge
- Gunner attack: Bruiser Formation or Biker Wedge
- Biker attack: Gunner Formation or Bruiser Wedge

## Choosing Formation — Against the Enemy

### Defense — what counters what:

- Strong Bruiser Attack → Biker Wedge
- Weak Bruiser Attack → Biker Formation
- Strong Gunner Attack → Bruiser Wedge
- Weak Gunner Attack → Bruiser Formation
- Strong Biker Attack → Gunner Wedge
- Weak Biker Attack → Gunner Formation

### Key rules:

- Formation: More kills, more losses
- Wedge: Fewer kills, fewer losses
- Every march should have disruption troops: 10-10-10 T2
- Every march should have a buffer of 10,000 troops one tier lower
