# Vision — #171 We are family

The clans PxU, CWS, 1Ca and PXU form a family with the following goals:

- Stronger presence in the Governor Battle
- Mutual support during the City Phase
- Shared growth
- Increasing attractiveness for new, active players

## Values & Code of Honor

### 1. Clan Autonomy

While the clans form a family, they remain independent and act together in the spirit of the shared vision. Each clan has its core members, individual rules, and independent leadership.

Maintaining autonomy ensures there is no "leveling" of individuality. Leaders should harness and promote the individuality of their clan members.

Autonomy is ensured through absolute transparency — all topics are discussed openly by and with the clan leadership, and debated together.

The family tolerates no envy or resentment, neither internally nor toward other clans. Violations of rules are sanctioned by the Family Council and may lead to exclusion from the entire family.

### 2. Unity and Cooperation

The family cooperates on all possible levels. This includes:

- Joint preparation (from building plans to distribution of buildings and rewards)
- Mutual support and security
- Clear communication and reliability

Internally and during the City Phase, all forms of support are welcome:

- Combat training by experienced players
- Help with events
- Unconditional defense against intruders
- Rotating events across the family clans

If a player wishes to join another family clan, this is generally tolerated. Clan hopping should be avoided. Active poaching within the family is strictly forbidden.

### 3. Clarity — Boundaries — Commitment

Being part of this family also means consistently adhering to agreed rules and commitments. Personal feelings, private disagreements, or envy have no place in this cooperation.

Fundamental principle: Harm to one member is harm to the community!

Mutual support, loyalty to the family, and respectful treatment of one another are basic requirements for all participating clans.
