# Correct Use of Weapons

Like formations, heroes are each assigned to a small group. 4 small hero groups go into battle per unit type. Hero assignment is based on troop type — if you field 5 Biker heroes, only 4 of them will deal damage.

## Weapon Types

Click the small "i" above a weapon to read its effect. There are three types:

- Weapons that boost the skills of their small hero group
- Weapons that boost the skills of ALL small hero groups
- Weapons that boost hero skills

## Weapon Assignment

Weapons that specifically support the small hero group should be assigned to heroes who will reliably deal damage.

Support weapons for all hero groups or general stats should be assigned to a hero who cannot be knocked out.

## Example — Pure Biker Raid

Bring one Bruiser or Gunner hero — ideally heroes with general stats like Red Thorns, El Santos, Tengu, Princess, Anonymus.

- Biker heroes: assign weapons that boost their small hero group
- General hero: assign weapon that boosts general stats

## Check the Battle Report

In the battle report, you can see how many times each weapon was used and how many troops it killed. Use this to verify whether your assignment was optimal.

## Important Note

You need to experiment to find which weapon gives the best results. The best place to practice is attacking Casinos — the enemies always have the same number and type of troops.

If a weapon cancels the hero ability (visible in the weapon description), it will not score any kills — even if it activates frequently.
