## 1. The Deposit System — Overview

The clan vault lives off the combined deposits of all members. Only those who deposit regularly can count on support when it matters — after a raid — to heal their troops.

The system is simple: **Deposit → Upload screenshot → done.** The app takes care of the rest.

---

## 2. How much do I need to deposit?

Every week, every member deposits **5M per resource** — for all 5 resource types. The amount is fixed and does not change.

> **Weekly minimum deposit: 5M × 5 resources = 25M total**

> 💡 The app automatically calculates your **deposit standing** — whether you are on track since you joined. This standing determines your payout eligibility after a raid.

---

## 3. Which resources count?

There are five resource types to be deposited weekly:

- 💵 **Cash**
- 🔫 **Arms**
- 📦 **Cargo**
- ⚙️ **Metal**
- 💎 **Diamonds**

---

## 4. How do I upload a screenshot?

1. Take a screenshot of your deposit in The Grand Mafia immediately after the transfer.
2. Open the **Deposits** tab in the app.
3. Tap **+ Add deposit** and select the resource type.
4. Upload your screenshot.
5. The app reads the amount automatically via OCR — quickly check whether the recognised value is correct and confirm.
6. Value incorrect? Tap **✕** to delete the screenshot and try again. If it keeps failing, enter the amount **manually** — the deposit will then be sent to the officer review queue.

> ⚠️ **Without a screenshot** a deposit cannot be accepted. The screenshot is your proof.

> ⚠️ **Screenshots must not be older than 4 days.** Older evidence will not be accepted — so upload promptly.

---

## 5. What happens after uploading?

Deposits are **automatically approved** as soon as the screenshot is uploaded and the amount is correctly recognised by the app.

**Manually entered amounts** always go to the **officer review queue** — they are also only valid with a screenshot as proof. An officer reviews the case and approves the deposit.

---

## 6. Exemptions & Excuses

Sometimes life doesn't go according to plan. If you are **temporarily unable to deposit** in a given week, contact an **R4** (Officer or Admin) in the game. They will then enter the exemption in the app.

With an approved exemption you will receive **no backlog entry** for that period. The exemption applies only to the agreed period, and you can see your current exemption status in the app via the **🛡️ badge**.

> Asking for help is not a weakness — the system is designed so that exemptions are fair and straightforward.

## 7. The Payout System — Overview

After a raid in which troops were injured, you can request **healing support** from the clan vault. The payout is calculated based on your losses and personally processed by **Bam bamm** — our only transporter in the game.

---

## 8. When am I eligible for a payout?

You are eligible for a payout when **all of the following conditions** are met:

| Condition | Details |
|---|---|
| ✅ Minimum losses | **≥ 10,000 injured T4+ troops** in the raid |
| ✅ Injured only, no dead | Dead troops do **not** count — only injured |
| ✅ Deposit obligation met | Your minimum deposits are up to date (Approved exemptions are taken into account) |

> 💡 **T4+** means Tier 4 or higher — lower troop tiers do not count towards payout eligibility.

---

## 9. How much will I receive?

The payout depends on the **number of your injured troops** and the **troop type** — because each troop type requires different resources to heal.

The rates per 10,000 injured are:

| Resource | Payout per 10,000 injured |
|:---:|:---:|
| 💵 Cash | 2.4M |
| 🔫 Arms | 2.4M |
| 📦 Cargo | 2.4M |
| ⚙️ Metal | 2.4M |
| 💎 Diamonds | 1.2M |

Which of these resources you actually receive depends on which troop types you used in the raid. The **Raid Leader** uploads the battle report in the app — the calculation then happens automatically based on the losses recorded in it.

### Example calculation

You had 20,000 injured Bruisers + 10,000 injured Hitmen (T4+):

| Resource | Calculation | Payout |
|---|---|:---:|
| 💵 Cash | 30,000 × 2.4M / 10,000 | **7.2M** |
| 🔫 Arms | 10,000 × 2.4M / 10,000 | **2.4M** |
| 📦 Cargo | 30,000 × 2.4M / 10,000 | **7.2M** |
| ⚙️ Metal | 20,000 × 2.4M / 10,000 | **4.8M** |
| 💎 Diamonds | 30,000 × 1.2M / 10,000 | **3.6M** |

> 💡 You don't need to calculate anything manually — the Raid Leader uploads the battle report and the app calculates your payout automatically.

## 10. Raid Leaders — Special Rules

Raid Leaders do important organisational work for the clan and are rewarded for it:

| Rule | Regular Member | Raid Leader |
|---|:---:|:---:|
| Deposit obligation | ✅ Yes | ❌ Exempt |
| Payout amount | 1× (standard) | **2× (double)** |
| Minimum losses (10,000 T4+) | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes (still applies) |

> Raid Leaders must therefore still have **at least 10,000 injured T4+ troops** to receive a payout — the exemption only applies to deposits, not to the minimum loss requirement.

---

## 11. Who processes the payout?

All payouts are handled by **Bam bamm** — he is the only player in the clan with the role of bank transporter and can transfer resources from the clan vault.

Once your payout request is recorded in the app, Bam bamm coordinates the transfer. Please be patient — he needs to be active in the game for every payout.

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Can I make multiple deposits in one week?**  
Yes! Multiple deposits of the same resource are added together. The only thing that matters is that the total amount reaches the minimum by the end of the week.

**What if my screenshot is rejected?**  
An officer will look into it and clarify with you. If in doubt, simply make a new deposit and upload a fresh screenshot.

**I only had 8,000 injured troops in the raid — do I get nothing?**  
Unfortunately not. The minimum threshold of 10,000 injured (T4+) is a requirement for the payout. Below this threshold no reimbursement is possible.

**Dead troops are not reimbursed — why?**  
The game distinguishes between dead troops (permanently lost) and injured troops (healable). The clan system only supports healing, not replacing the dead.

**I forgot to deposit — what now?**  
Simply catch up or contact an Admin or Officer. For past weeks without an exemption a backlog entry will be created — but most things can be resolved in a conversation.

**How do I see my current status?**  
In the app under the **Deposits** tab you can see: your progress for the current week, your deposit history, any backlog entries, and whether you have an exemption.

**I forgot my password — what should I do?**  
Contact an **R4** (Officer or Admin) in the game and tell them your **username** (not your in-game name — the username is the one you chose when registering). The R4 will then reset your password via the app. You will receive a new password and can log in again immediately.
