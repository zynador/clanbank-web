# Unlocking T4

## Basics

The Investment Center needs Level 25 to research Level 10. This requires all buildings to be at Level 25 at least once.

### Special buildings:

- Clan Hall → requires Whiskey
- Prison → requires Handcuffs
- Secret Room → requires Shackles

Tip: Try to buy ingredients using Gold through each building's menu — you get significantly more per 10,000 Gold than through other means!

Gold should be spent almost exclusively on Whiskey, Handcuffs and Shackles.

## Investments

- The Economy branch must be at 100%
- All attacks etc. in the Armament branch must be at Level 10

### Costs:

- Level 10 Attack costs approximately 650 million Diamonds
- Level 10 Attack takes around 650 days with approximately 340% investment bonus

## Tips to Speed Things Up

- Once production of other resources is sufficient: only collect Diamond tiles
- Boost investment speed wherever possible!
- Save Buff items (10% and 20%) for the Level 10 investment
- Get the City Hall buff → 10%
- Upgrade investment equipment → up to 100% bonus (the 3 belts/jewelry alone give 75%)
- Set Talent Trees (Normal and GW) before investing → up to 115%
- Level up Hero Nioh → 25%
- Level up Hero Bonecrusher → 10%
- Investment Center Level 25 gives 30%
- Don't forget to level up your Babes — Babe 3 (Julia) gives 10%

It can be worth researching the Babe Investment tree as this speeds up Babe growth significantly.
